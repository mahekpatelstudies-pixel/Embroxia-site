Our story

Built by a designer,
for every designer.

Embroxia exists because professional embroidery tools should be accessible to everyone — not just those who can afford expensive Windows-only software.

Embroidery is one of the oldest crafts in the world. From ancient imperial robes to modern sportswear, thread and needle have told human stories for thousands of years. Yet the software that professional embroidery designers rely on in 2026 feels like it was built in 2006 — expensive, complex, Windows-only, and completely disconnected from how modern designers actually work.

Embroxia was built to change that. We started with a simple question: what would embroidery software look like if it were designed today, from scratch, for every kind of creator — the freelance digitizer in Mumbai, the fashion designer in Paris, the small embroidery shop in Texas, the garment manufacturer in Guangzhou?

"Professional embroidery tools should be accessible to every designer in the world — not just those who can afford $2,000+ software that only runs on Windows."

The answer is Embroxia. Browser-based, AI-powered, globally accessible, and priced for everyone from hobbyists to manufacturers. No install. No dongle. No Windows requirement. Just open your browser and start designing.

We're still early. The product is growing every week. But we believe deeply that the future of embroidery design is open, intelligent, and collaborative — and we're building that future one stitch at a time.
