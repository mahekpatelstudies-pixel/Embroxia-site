Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to run Embroxia. We never sell your data. We never show you ads. You can delete your data anytime by emailing us.

1. Who we are

Embroxia ("we", "us", "our") is a browser-based embroidery design platform operated by Mahek Patel. Our website is located at embroxia.com. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at mahekpatel@embroxia.com.

2. What information we collect

Information you give us

  • Email address — when you sign up for early access or the waitlist
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy. We never see or store your card details
  • Designs you create — stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not upload your designs to our servers unless you explicitly save them to a cloud account

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, time spent (anonymized)
  • Browser and device type — to ensure compatibility
  • IP address — for security and fraud prevention only

3. How we use your information

  • To send you product updates and announcements (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To process payments via Lemon Squeezy
  • To improve the platform based on how it's used
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than running Embroxia.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the platform to function — session management and preference storage. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings but some features may not work correctly.

5. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing (lemonsqueezy.com/privacy)
  • Netlify — website hosting (netlify.com/privacy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI assistant features (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Brevo — email communications (brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy)

6. Data retention

We retain your email address and account information for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days. Designs stored locally in your browser are under your full control and are not retained by us.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email us at mahekpatel@embroxia.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's privacy

Embroxia is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. All data transmission is encrypted via SSL/TLS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
mahekpatel@embroxia.com
